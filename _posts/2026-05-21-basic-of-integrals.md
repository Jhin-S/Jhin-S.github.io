## 1. Introduction to Integrals

### Learning Objectives (Outcomes)
* **Interpret** the value of an integral within a practical, real-world context.
* **Describe** the relationship between an integral and the concept of area, or the continuous compounding/accumulation of a process.
* **Parse and understand** the standard mathematical notation associated with integrals.
* **Compute** the integrals of functions that possess geometrically simple graphs.
* **Explain** the formal definition of an integral in terms of area under a curve or Riemann sums.

---

## 2. Integral Notation

Understanding the anatomy of an integral is the first step to mastering it. Here is the standard definite integral notation and its components:

$$\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx$$

* $\int$ : **Integral sign** (적분 기호)
* $b$ : **Upper bound** (위끝)
* $a$ : **Lower bound** (아래끝)
* $f(x)$ : **Integrand** (피적분함수 - 적분 당하는 함수)
* $dx$ : Indicates the variable of integration
